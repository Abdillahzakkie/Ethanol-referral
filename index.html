Referral System

The WISE contract has a direct, one-level referral system that rewards both the referrer and referee when a user reserves WISE tokens.

Rewards that are paid in WISE are based on how much total ETH your referral link brought into the WISE liquidity transformer smart contract:

  • Refer 1 - 49.999 ETH

    Reward = 0.05 ETH (paid in DYY)

  • Refer 50 or more ETH

    Reward = 10% of ETH (paid in DYY)
